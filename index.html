FAQ

Quick Answers

What's the minimum order?

Most items start at 6 pieces minimum. Some products have lower minimums — just ask!

How long does it take?

Standard orders ship in 2-3 weeks. Need it faster? Rush orders (1-2 weeks) available for an additional fee.

Can you match specific colors?

Yep! Send us your colors (Pantone, hex codes, or even a photo) and we'll match them as closely as possible.

Do you help with design?

Absolutely. Send us your ideas, Pinterest boards, or rough sketches and we'll create a custom design for you to approve.

Can everyone order different sizes?

Of course! We collect sizes from your crew and make sure everyone gets what fits them best.

Can you ship to multiple addresses?

Yes! We can ship to one address or directly to each person in your group.
